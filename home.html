Our Chai Journey

Chai Casa was born from a love for the rich traditions of Indian chai and the vibrant artistry of Talavera pottery. Our founder, Priya Sharma, grew up in Mumbai watching her grandmother brew masala chai in handcrafted clay pots, a ritual that filled their home with warmth and connection.

After moving to Calgary, Priya longed to recreate that sense of community and the authentic taste of home. She combined her passion for Indian tea culture with the beautiful Talavera ceramics she discovered during travels to Mexico, recognizing how both traditions celebrate craftsmanship, vibrant colors, and the joy of sharing.

Every cup at Chai Casa is brewed using traditional techniques - boiling Assam tea leaves with fresh spices in milk to extract maximum flavor. We serve our chai in authentic Talavera pottery, each piece hand-painted by artisans in Puebla, Mexico, creating a unique fusion of cultures in every cup.

From the hand-pounded spices of traditional Indian masala chai to the intricate patterns of our Talavera serveware, every detail at Chai Casa is crafted with intention and care. We believe chai is more than a beverage—it's a moment of connection, a pause in your day, a warm embrace.

"One Cup, Two Cultures" - this is our promise to bring you an authentic chai experience that honors both Indian tradition and Mexican artistry in the heart of Calgary.
